Sport

I play Gaa with Macroom Ladies football club.We train every Wednesday night from 6:30pm-8:00pm. I've played football for 8 years. The GAA was founded on November 1st 1884 at a meeting in Thurles, Co. Tipperary, by a group of spirited Irishmen who had the foresight to realise the importance of establishing a national organisation to make athletics more accessible to the masses and to revive and nurture traditional,indigenous sports and pastimes. At that time, it was largely only the gentry and aristocracy who were allowed to meaningfully participate in athletics.

Travel

I love travelling both at home and abroad. I have been to most counties in Ireland and I've loved them all. I'm from Cork but my favouriyte county by far is Co. Dublin. There is such a busy atmosphere in Dublin city, with people rushing around and there is shops everywhere. Dublin has such a good nightlife and is an ideal location for shopping! Dublin has so many attactions and mueseums to visist, my favourite being Dublin zoo.I've been to a few countries and have had the most amazing experiences there. I've been to Germany,France,Spain,England,Scotland,Austria, and Georgia. A dream I have is to one day travel the world. Some of the countries I'd love to experience include Australia, America , Canada and South Africa.
